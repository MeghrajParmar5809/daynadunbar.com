The Counseling Experience

These sessions take place in person or on Zoom. Beginning with an invocation to call in Spirit’s assistance, Dayna creates a safe container for the deep exploration of the source of the client’s suffering. Approaching the work as a deeply nurturing presence as well as a detective of the psyche, Dayna zeroes in on the mental and emotional knots that keep her clients imprisoned in inner struggle. Dayna calls her approach Two Pillars of Awakening and Healing—Love and Wisdom.

She offers the alchemy that occurs when we meet our humanity fully in love while we also do the waking up work offered by perennial spiritual Wisdom. In this way, she supports the shift in identity from a separate self-structure to that of Infinite Being and Oneness in Spirit.
