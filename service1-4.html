Group Retreats and Spiritual Pilgrimages

Home Service Shamanic Group Retreats

Dayna hosts these retreats and pilgrimages as sacred invitations to journey inward — through land, breath, and community. Whether you’re seeking transformation through group energy or longing to walk the earth in spiritual remembrance, these immersive experiences awaken your spirit, open your heart, and reconnect you with your truth.

Group Retreats

Come home to yourself — in community.

Dayna’s residential group retreats are deeply immersive experiences designed to support healing, embodiment, and connection. Held in sacred natural locations, these retreats are more than a getaway — they’re a portal into transformation.

You’ll journey through:

  • Breathwork and guided shamanic journeys to access altered states and release
  • Yoga and pranayama to bring the body into presence and flow
  • Tantric practices that gently open pathways of intimacy within the group
  • Dyads and relational work to deepen your capacity for conscious communication
  • Excursions into deep nature, allowing the Earth to hold and mirror your process

These retreats are spaces of deep remembrance. Of your body. Of your truth. Of the medicine that arises when we gather in circle with intention and presence.

Spiritual Pilgrimages

Walk the land. Awaken the soul.

Some journeys aren’t just about where you go — but who you become as you experience them.

Dayna’s spiritual pilgrimages are sacred experiences designed for those ready to meet the soul of a land — and their own soul — through ritual, practice, and presence. These journeys invite reverence, slowness, and spiritual depth.

In 2026, Dayna will lead a pilgrimage to Rishikesh, India — the sacred Himalayan city known as the birthplace of yoga and a place where sages, seekers, and mystics have gathered for centuries.

This is not a retreat. It’s a pilgrimage — a devotional walk into the unknown, into beauty, and into something far greater than the self.

You’ll experience:

  • Daily embodied practices
  • Sacred temple visits and fire rituals
  • Cultural immersion and connection to lineage
  • Time on the banks of the Ganga river
  • A deep dive into the spiritual heart of India

Whether in the stillness of a forest retreat or the vibrant colors of an ancient city, Dayna holds you through every step — guiding you home to your own inner sanctuary.
