Dayna Dunbar

Spiritual & Therapeutic Counselor

Dayna Dunbar is a counselor and retreat facilitator who holds a master's degree in Spiritual Psychology from the University of Santa Monica (2001). She specializes in supporting clients with acute, chronic, and/or developmental trauma from childhood, intuitively blending modalities and approaches like Psychosynthesis, Somatic Experiencing, Internal Family Systems, Gestalt Psychology, Inner Child Reparenting, and Non-dual teachings. All of this is in the context of and woven through with the understanding that the healing comes when we shift identity from the egoic self- structure to the infinite Truth of our own loving Being.

Shamanic Journey Counselor

For the last several years, Dayna has been supporting clients using the ancient shamanic and current cutting-edge practice in psychology—sacred journey work. She has guided clients on more than 60 journeys, trained under a mentor in Boulder, CO, who has been doing this work or more than a dozen years. Dayna has also taken workshops and courses from PESI and The Heart Mind Institute.

Author

Dayna is also an award-winning spiritual novelist published by Ballantine Books. Her visionary storytelling, as illustrated in her latest book, Awake:The Legacy of Akara, offers an entertaining, fast-paced adventure and a vision of what we may become if we choose the path of conscious evolution.

Personal background

Dayna’s healing and awakening path began as they so often do—because of great suffering. In addition to severe childhood trauma, Dayna began to experience debilitating chronic illness as a freshman in college and found that Western medicine was not working. As an agnostic at the time, it was only through this ungentle push that she began searching for alternative modalities and found acupuncture, energy healing, holistic health practices, meditation and the idea of a mind/body/spirit connection.

Dayna began a regular meditation practice at nineteen and since then has pursued, through all of the approaches she now teaches, the highest potential of the Earth experience—realizing the spiritual Truth of Self and of all Being.
