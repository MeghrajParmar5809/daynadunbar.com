About Dayna

Dayna Dunbar is a spiritual and therapeutic counselor and retreat facilitator who holds a master's degree in Spiritual Psychology from the University of Santa Monica (2001). She specializes in supporting clients with acute, chronic, and/or developmental trauma from childhood, intuitively blending modalities and approaches like Psychosynthesis, Somatic Experiencing, Internal Family Systems, Gestalt Psychology, Inner Child Reparenting, and Non-dual teachings. All of this is in the context of and woven through with the understanding that the healing comes when we shift identity from the egoic self- structure to the infinite Truth of our own loving Being.

For the last several years, Dayna has been supporting clients using the ancient shamanic and current cutting-edge practice in psychology—sacred journey work. She has trained under a mentor in Boulder, CO, who has been guiding shamanic journeys for more than a dozen years. Dayna has taken workshops and courses for this work from PESI and The Heart Mind Institute.
